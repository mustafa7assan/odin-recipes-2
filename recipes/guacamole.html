Guacamole

Description

A creamy avocado-based dip with lime, cilantro, and onions.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime juice
  • Cilantro
  • Red onion
  • Jalapeño
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mash avocados.
  2. Stir in lime juice, chopped cilantro, red onion, and jalapeño.
  3. Season with salt to taste.
