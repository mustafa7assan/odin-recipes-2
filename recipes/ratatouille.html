Ratatouille

Description

A French vegetable stew with eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Eggplant.
  • Zucchini
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs (thyme, basil)

Steps

  1. Slice all vegetables.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add bell peppers, cook until soft.
  4. Add eggplant and zucchini, cook until tender.
  5. Add tomatoes and herbs, simmer until all vegetables are cooked through.
